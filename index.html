what we do

We provide support, guidance, information and training for every stage of your English language career. Whatever next step you are dreaming of we can help you get there.

why we do it

We love teaching English and want to share this with as many people as possible! It can be a great career, an awesome way to travel, to meet new people and to experience different cultures. Working as an English language teacher online can be a good way to build a flexible work schedule that works for your life. However, there is so much information out there how do you know where to look and what is reliable?

how we do it

We provide a range of products and services to help you work out where your ELT career is headed and how to get there. These currently include:

  • Blog articles on common topics and signposting to great places for information that already exist
  • Free downloads to help with career planning
  • Mini courses on specific areas of teacher development
  • Business support if you want to set up freelance
  • Marketing strategy consulations
  • Custom coded websites for your solo projects
  • More in depth courses, including "Teaching with Technology" coming soon
  • A Facebook community of like-minded teachers who are working towards their dreams
